Layered Beef Casserole

Layered Beef Casserole Recipe photo by Taste of Home

On my busy days, I treasure meal-in-one recipes like this. Toss together a salad and dinner is ready in no time. --Dorothy Wiedeman, Eaton, Colarado Ingredients
  • 6 medium potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 can of (15-1/4 ounces) whole kernel corn, drained
  • 1/2 cup chopped green pepper
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cups sliced fresh carrots
  • 1-1/2 pounds of lean ground beef (90%)lean
  • 1 can (8 ounces) tomato sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup shredded Velveeta
Directions
  1. Layer the Potato, corn, green pepper, onion and carrots.
  2. Crumble the beef over the vegetables.
  3. Pour tomato sauce over the top.Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook for 2 hours at 350 degree C until meat is no longer pink and thermometer reads 160 degree C
  5. Sprinkle with cheest, let stand for 10min before serving
